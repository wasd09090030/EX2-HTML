The Characters
Shinnosuke Nohara (Shinchan) Misae Nohara (Mom) Hiroshi Nohara (Dad)

The youngest of the Noharas. Loves shiny objects and brandname goods. Smiles sideways, like Shinchan The family dog — small, fluffy, and sometimes the most sensible member of the household. The smart and responsible friend of Shinchan, often the voice of reason in the group.
